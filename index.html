Research Interests

My research interests lie in Multi-modal Learning, Generative Models (including Multimodal LLMs and Diffusion Models) and Video Understanding. Currently, I'm focusing on advancing multi-modal unerstanding and generation through the integration of Large Language Models (LLMs) and Diffusion Models (DMs), aiming to connect and leverage the latent representation spaces of these two model architectures.

I'm looking for self-motivated graduate/ungraduate students to collaborate with. Don't hesitate to reach out to me if you are interested.
